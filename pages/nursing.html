Nursing Unit

Introduction

This unit is the largest with large numbers of personnels to one of the component organs of the centre, and has responsility for meeting the nursing needs of the University Community and its environs. The Unit renders its services/oversees other satelite clinics.

These are - MAin Campus, Kongo Campus, I.A.R., NAPRI, SBRS Funtua, DAC, Clinics Samaru, Mando Kaduna, Kabba, then Samaru clinic where OTS (Others) in the Community are seen.

The Unit render direct services to the public in addition to certain specialized services (clinics), such as:

  • O & G
  • Paediatrics
  • Psychiatry (Mental Health)
  • Ophthalmology (Eye)
  • E.T.N.
  • Medical
  • Surgical
  • T.B.L.
  • A.N.C. / I.W.C.
  • S.S. Clinic

Consultants do come from ABUTH to the inpatient in these clinics assigned by nurses.

Staff Strength

Main Campus 44 Nurses (11 CNOs, 2 Contract CNOs)
Kongo Campus 15 Nurses
I.A.R. 2 Nurses
NAPRI 2 Nurses
Mando 1 Nurse (CNO)
Kabba 1 Nurse (PNO)
DAC Samaru 1 Nurse (CHO)
SBRS Funtua 1 Nurse (CNO) Contract
Training / Course 2 Nurses on Part-time
1 Nurse on Sabbatical appointment
1 Nurse on Leave of Absent
6 Nurses on Various courses

The staff strength above is in adequate to cater for the twenty four hours shifting duties, public holidays and other emergencies that may arise. Thus, there is need to add more hands.
