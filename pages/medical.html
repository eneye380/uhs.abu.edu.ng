Medical Unit

Introduction

Medical Record Unit is the first point of call and the service rendered by the unit is felt across all speciality in the hospital as such politeness, tolerance are expected from the medical record personnel to project the image of hospital because the first impression matters a lot toward what service will be rendered to patient.

Activities

  • Registration of patient both old and new.
  • Retrieving of patient information when required.
  • Preservation of patient record.
  • Compilation of hospital statistics i.e. Monthly and Annually.
  • Provide data to protect the legal interest of the patient.
  • Filling of patient record.
  • Compilation of daily NHIS Statistics.
  • Attending to student on practical attachment ( IT ).
  • Sourcing of patient record to various special clinics.
  • Weeding of inactive record.
  • Confidentiality of Medical Record.
  • Provide totality of patient medical history in the hospital.
  • Storage of health information etc.
